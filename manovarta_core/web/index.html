Behind the scenes

How ManoVarta works

ManoVarta is designed to feel simple on the surface while a structured screening flow runs quietly underneath.

1. Listen naturally People can type or speak in English, Hindi, or Hinglish.
2. Choose the next best question The planner looks for missing evidence and asks the smallest helpful follow-up.
3. Build a structured picture The backend maps open-ended conversation to questionnaire-style symptom signals.
4. Keep safety first Safety monitors can slow or redirect the flow if the conversation sounds high-risk.
