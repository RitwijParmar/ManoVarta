How ManoVarta works

Simple on the surface, structured underneath

ManoVarta tries to feel as calm as a journal or companion app, while a structured screening flow runs quietly in the background.

1. Start naturally You can type or speak in English, Hindi, or Hinglish.
2. Follow the strongest signal The system asks the smallest helpful follow-up instead of forcing a rigid checklist.
3. Build a quiet structured picture Open-ended conversation is translated into questionnaire-aligned evidence in the background.
4. Slow down for safety when needed If something sounds risky, the conversation becomes more careful and support-forward.
