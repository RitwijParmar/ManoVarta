Architecture

How ManoVarta works

ManoVarta is designed as a complete product: calm frontend, adaptive conversation brain, multilingual inference, and safety checks running together in one flow.

1. Listen naturally The frontend captures typed or spoken responses in English, Hindi, or Hinglish.
2. Plan the next question The backend dialogue planner decides what to ask next based on missing evidence and confidence.
3. Infer and structure The inference engine maps open-ended conversation into questionnaire-aligned symptom evidence.
4. Protect the user Safety checks watch for emotional escalation or self-harm risk and can interrupt the normal flow.
